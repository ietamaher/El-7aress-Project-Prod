\chapter*{WARNINGS AND CAUTIONS}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Warnings and Cautions}

\WARNING{%
\textbf{LETHAL WEAPON SYSTEM}\\[5pt]
This system controls a live weapon platform. Failure to follow safety procedures can result in death or serious injury. Always treat the weapon as if it is loaded. Maintain positive identification of targets before engaging. Verify no friendly forces are in the line of fire.
}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\CAUTION{%
\textbf{ELECTRICAL HAZARDS}\\[5pt]
The RCWS operates on 24-28 VDC at up to 40 amperes. Disconnect all power sources before performing any maintenance. Only qualified personnel shall perform electrical servicing.
}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\WARNING{%
\textbf{MOVING PARTS}\\[5pt]
The gimbal system contains high-speed rotating components. Keep clear of moving parts during operation. Emergency stop must be accessible at all times.
}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\WARNING{%
\textbf{LASER HAZARD}\\[5pt]
The Laser Range Finder emits Class 1M laser radiation at 1550nm. Do not view directly with optical instruments. Ensure range safety protocols are followed during operations.
}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\CAUTION{%
\textbf{EQUIPMENT DAMAGE - GIMBAL LIMITS}\\[5pt]
Operating the gimbal beyond mechanical limits (-20\degree~to +60\degree~elevation) may cause permanent damage to servos and encoders. Always observe gimbal position indicators. Do not force manual movement when power is applied.
}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\CAUTION{%
\textbf{ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION}\\[5pt]
Thermal and day cameras are sensitive to extreme temperatures. Operating outside -20\degree C to +60\degree C ambient range may degrade performance or cause damage. Protect system from moisture, dust, and salt spray when not in use.
}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\NOTE{%
Throughout this manual, the following conventions apply:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=2em,itemsep=2pt]
\item \textbf{WARNING} indicates hazards that could result in death or serious injury
\item \textbf{CAUTION} indicates hazards that could result in equipment damage
\item \textbf{NOTE} provides additional information or clarification
\item \button{Button X} indicates joystick or control panel buttons
\item \osd{TEXT} indicates on-screen display elements
\end{itemize}
}

\subsection*{Course Description}

This course qualifies military operators to safely and effectively operate the El 7arress Remote Controlled Weapon Station (RCWS). Students will learn system operation, targeting procedures, tracking techniques, ballistic compensation, emergency procedures, and live fire engagement.

\subsection*{Learning Outcomes}

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Safely operate the El 7arress RCWS in all environmental conditions
    \item Perform pre-operation checks and system status verification
    \item Execute target acquisition, tracking, and engagement procedures
    \item Apply ballistic compensation for various ammunition types and ranges
    \item Respond to emergency situations and system malfunctions
    \item Perform operator-level maintenance and troubleshooting
    \item Conduct post-operation procedures and documentation
\end{enumerate}

\section*{Emergency Actions}

\begin{longtable}{@{}lp{10cm}@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Emergency} & \textbf{Immediate Action} \\
\midrule
\endhead
Personnel in danger & \textbf{STRIKE E-STOP BUTTON IMMEDIATELY} \\
Wrong target tracked & Double-click \button{Button 4} ($<$500ms) to abort tracking \\
Accidental discharge & Release \button{Button 5} (Fire), Release \button{Button 0} (Master Arm) \\
Runaway gimbal & \textbf{E-STOP} → Station Power OFF \\
Fire or smoke & E-STOP → Station OFF → Main Power OFF → Evacuate \\
\bottomrule
\end{longtable}

\vspace{1cm}

\section*{Pre-Operation Checklist}

\begin{checklist}
\item Main Power ON, verify power indicator
\item Station Enable ON, verify station active
\item All devices connected (System Status check - all green)
\item Day camera operational (clear image, proper exposure)
\item Thermal camera operational (image quality acceptable)
\item Gimbal smooth motion test (azimuth 360\degree, elevation -20\degree~to +60\degree)
\item Laser Range Finder test (returns valid range)
\item Tracking acquisition test (lock on test target)
\item Weapon Arm/Safe switches functional
\item Active ballistic zero loaded (\osd{Z} indicator visible on OSD)
\item Emergency Stop button accessible and functional
\item Communication with command post established
\end{checklist}
